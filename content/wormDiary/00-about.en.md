> This section does not represent my professional identity as a Computer Science student or programmer. It reflects how I experience and think about the world as a person.

Writing is the way I interpret the world and dissolve the confusions of living. I am drawn to abstraction, to metaphors that circle my own lived experiences, resisting the unspeakable density and absurdity of being embodied.

This section gathers some of my writing: mainly ***Worm Diaries***, along with a few poems.

The Worm Diary began in 2025 as a series of short pieces. I imagined a worm who can ride a bicycle, and I followed what it might see on the road, what it might encounter, what kinds of feeling might quietly take shape within it.

They are the worm's diaries.

They are mine.

They might belong to many other folks as well.

---

*I primarily write in Chinese. The English versions are translated with the assistance of ChatGPT.*

*Because Chinese and English differ fundamentally in structure, rhythm, and cultural resonance, translation is often an inherently difficult process. For this reason, the English texts largely follow the original semantic meaning rather than attempting to recreate every nuance of the original piece.*
